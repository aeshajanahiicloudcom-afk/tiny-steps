About Us

A Second Home
for Your Child

At Tiny Steps, we believe every child is unique. We blend play with learning to nurture emotional, social, and physical development in a safe, loving environment.

  • Safe, secure & fully equipped environment
  • Healthy, nutritious meals provided daily
  • Qualified, caring & passionate teachers
  • Child-centred holistic curriculum
  • Small class sizes for personal attention
  • Regular parent updates & communication
